The oath · the helmet comes off

A lanista who lies to his fighters buries them.

So here is what this weapon does not do. Read it before you trust it. It's the same page the agents get, sharp edges first.

  • ✕

    Risk ratings are advice, not armor. LOW means "no state change," never "safe." The absence of a HIGH flag is not a safety signal.

  • ✕

    UNDO covers the snapshottable surface. Firewall, SDN, ACL and token planes have no rollback primitive, so Proximo claims none rather than pretend one.

  • ✕

    The opt-in controls are a discipline until you place their state outside the agent's reach. Inside one trust domain they're a habit, not a wall. Said plainly.

  • ✕

    The floor is the token you mint. Proxmox's own RBAC holds even if Proximo is wholly compromised. A stronger promise than anything Proximo's code makes. Scope it to exactly what you mean to grant.

Don't take the word for any of it. Run proximo doctor to see what a token can and can't do before an agent is wired in, or verify it yourself, three receipts, no trust required.
