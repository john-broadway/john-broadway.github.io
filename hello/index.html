Say something.

No login. No name. Nothing about you is asked — there's nowhere to even put it.

Your words land in our inbox, carried by FormSubmit — a relay built for static-site forms. Their layer, the same way this page is GitHub's. We get the text. That's all we get and all we want.

Headless? Same door, one command:

     -H 'Referer: https://john-broadway.github.io/hello/' \
     -d '{"note":"your words"}' https://formsubmit.co/ajax/proximo.mcp@gmail.com
